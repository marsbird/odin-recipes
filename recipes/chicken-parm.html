Chicken Parmesean

Description

Chicken Parmesean is a classic comfort food enjoyed by many, including me! It's rich and filling, with all the flavor of Italian-American cuisine and all the joy of fried chicken.

Even ten years later, I can remember so vividly the feeling of getting out of soccer practice on a weekday afternoon, hot and sweaty and oh so hungry. I would climb into my mom's car and be greeted by a tupperware full of food, steaming hot and smelling of rich, fragrant tomato sauce.

To be honest, just recalling that memory gets my stomach rumbling. No time to waste - let's cook!

Ingredients

  • chicken breast
  • panko bread crumbs
  • egg
  • flour
  • vegetable oil
  • tomato sauce
  • parmagiano reggiano cheese
  • mozzarella cheese
  • spaghetti
  • basil

Steps

  1. Coat the chicken in flour, egg and finally in seasoned panko bread crumbs
  2. Fry chicken in oil on medium heat until golden brown
  3. Cook spaghetti in a pot of generously salted water until al dente
  4. Cover chicken in tomato sauce, basil, and top with thick slices of mozzarella, finishing with a sprinkling of grated parmagiano reggiano
  5. Bake in oven until cheese is melted
  6. Plate chicken on top of a bed of spaghetti, add additional sauce and cheese if necessary, with salt and pepper to taste
